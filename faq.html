Frequently Asked Questions

What is a mobile signal booster?

A mobile signal booster is a device that amplifies weak mobile signals to provide better coverage and call quality in areas with poor reception. It works by capturing the existing signal, amplifying it, and rebroadcasting it to improve connectivity.

How do I know if I need a signal booster?

You might need a signal booster if you experience dropped calls, poor call quality, slow internet speeds, or no signal in certain areas of your home or building. Check your signal strength using your phone's signal bars or network settings.

Are signal boosters legal to use?

Yes, signal boosters are legal in the UK when they comply with Ofcom regulations. Our products are certified and meet all legal requirements for use in the United Kingdom.

Which mobile networks are supported?

Our boosters support all major UK networks including Vodafone, O2, EE, Three, giffgaff, Tesco Mobile, BT, and Virgin. We offer both single-network and multi-network solutions.

Do boosters work with 5G networks?

Yes, we offer 5G-compatible boosters that support the latest network technology. However, 5G coverage varies by location, so check your area's 5G availability before purchasing.

Will a booster work with my phone?

Signal boosters work with all mobile phones, regardless of brand or model. They amplify the signal at the network level, so compatibility depends on your network provider, not your device.

How difficult is installation?

Most installations are straightforward and can be completed in 1-2 hours. We provide detailed installation guides and video tutorials. For complex setups or commercial installations, we recommend professional installation services.

What tools do I need for installation?

Basic tools include a drill, screwdriver, cable clips, and possibly a ladder. All necessary mounting hardware and cables are included with your booster kit.

Where should I place the outdoor antenna?

The outdoor antenna should be mounted as high as possible (roof, chimney, or wall) where it can receive the strongest signal. Avoid placing it near metal objects or other antennas that could cause interference.

How much coverage can I expect?

Coverage depends on the booster model and your specific signal conditions. Our range includes solutions for small spaces (300 sqm) up to large commercial buildings (5000+ sqm). Check our product finder quiz for personalized recommendations.

Will a booster improve my internet speed?

Yes, a signal booster can significantly improve data speeds by providing a stronger, more stable connection. This is especially noticeable in areas with weak signal strength.

How long does it take to see improvements?

Improvements are typically immediate once installation is complete and the booster is powered on. However, it may take a few minutes for the system to fully optimize and provide maximum performance.

What if my booster isn't working properly?

First, check that all connections are secure and the power light is on. Ensure proper antenna placement and separation between indoor and outdoor antennas. Our troubleshooting guide covers common issues, or contact our support team for assistance.

Why is my signal still weak after installation?

Weak signals can result from insufficient outdoor signal strength, improper antenna placement, or interference. Try repositioning the outdoor antenna or contact us for a signal strength assessment.

Can weather affect my booster?

Extreme weather conditions can temporarily affect signal quality, but our outdoor antennas are designed to withstand UK weather. Heavy rain or snow may cause minor signal fluctuations.

What warranty do you provide?

All our products come with a comprehensive warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Warranty terms vary by product - check individual product pages for specific details.

How can I get technical support?

We offer multiple support channels including email, phone, and live chat. Our technical team is available to help with installation, troubleshooting, and any questions you may have.

Do you offer installation support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive installation support including guides, videos, and technical assistance. For commercial installations or complex setups, we can recommend certified installers in your area.

How long does shipping take?

Standard UK delivery typically takes 2-3 business days. We also offer express delivery options for faster shipping. International shipping times vary by destination.

What if I'm not satisfied with my purchase?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee with easy returns. If you're not completely satisfied, you can return your booster within the specified return period for a full refund or replacement.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to many countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Contact us for specific international shipping information.
