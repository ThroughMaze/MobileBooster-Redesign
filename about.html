Our Journey

A Decade of Excellence in Signal Enhancement

Mobile Booster was founded in 2014 when our CEO experienced firsthand the frustration of poor mobile signal in his rural home office. After struggling with dropped calls and slow data speeds that affected his business, he discovered signal boosters and was amazed by the transformation.

Recognizing that millions of people across the UK faced similar connectivity challenges, he assembled a team of telecommunications experts and engineers to create the most comprehensive mobile signal enhancement solutions available.

Today, we're proud to be the UK's most trusted name in mobile signal boosters, serving everyone from individual homeowners to large enterprises with cutting-edge technology and unparalleled customer support.
